About Me

I am a passionate web developer and computer science student at the University of Zambia, specializing in creating responsive, accessible, and user-centered web experiences. My journey into web development began with a curiosity about how websites function, which quickly evolved into a dedication to mastering HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

I enjoy solving complex problems through clean code and thoughtful design, and I'm particularly interested in inclusive design practices that ensure digital products work for everyone. Beyond coding, I actively explore modern CSS techniques like Grid and Flexbox to build flexible layouts that adapt seamlessly across devices.

My goal is to contribute to meaningful projects that make a positive impact while continuing to grow as a well-rounded developer. What sets me apart is my commitment to lifelong learning, attention to detail, and belief that technology should empower communities and bridge digital divides.
